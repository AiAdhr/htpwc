Personal Favourite

x3 Schmietta, simply because she's our best small witch
x2 Genni, usefull if you want to have access to a particular witchcrafter spell effect within one turn
x1 Potterie, she's really usefull when you want to retrieve Patronus or Draping so you can grab it for the next round instead of waiting to retrieve it at the end of the next round
x1 Pittore, basically a free draw if you already have a witchcrafter spell in hand that you won't use this turn
x3 Reasoning, probably the best non-Witchcrafter spell for your deck, a free summon + the chance to have many Witchcrafter spell card dropped into the GY which you'll get at the end of the turn
