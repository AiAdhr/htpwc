Witchcrafter Patronus

CONTINUOUS TRAP
You can target 1 of your Spellcaster monsters that is banished or in your GY; shuffle it into the Deck, and if you do, add 1 "Witchcrafter" Spell from your Deck to your hand.
If this card is in your GY, except the turn it was sent there: You can banish this card, then target any number of your banished "Witchcrafter" Spells with different names; add them to your hand.
You can only use each effect of "Witchcrafter Patronus" once per turn.

Witchcrafter Masterpiece

NORMAL TRAP
If you control a "Witchcrafter" monster: Target 1 Spell in either GY; add 1 card with the same name from your Deck to your hand.
During either player's turn, except the turn this card was sent to the GY: You can banish this card and any number of Spells from your GY; Special Summon 1 "Witchcrafter" monster from your Deck whose Level equals the number of Spells banished to activate this effect.
You can only use each effect of "Witchcrafter Masterpiece" once per turn.
